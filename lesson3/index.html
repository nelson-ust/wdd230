Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is educates pet owners who have disabled or otherwise impaired pets. It will also focus on informing new or prospective pet parents on how to best care for adopted pets from shelters and other situations by offering tips and tricks to help you better care for your pet. This site will also provide more information to other bloggers dedicated to helping dog owners find the most up-to-date information on pets including reviews and scientific research on various areas.
