Our Story

Bountiful Foods was founded in 2008 by John and Mary Smith with the mission to provide fresh and healthy food options to the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas of southern California.

We are committed to sourcing organic, locally grown produce directly from farmers in the area. In addition, we work with national and international suppliers to bring the best fruits and produce to our customers.

Our storefront is located in Carlsbad, California, but we also operate several food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.
